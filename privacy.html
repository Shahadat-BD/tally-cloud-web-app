Privacy Policy

Introduction

Tally Cloud is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our website and services (the "Service").

What Personal Information Do We Collect?

We collect the following types of personal information from you:

Contact information: This includes your name, email address, and phone number.

Account information: This includes your username, password, and billing information.

Usage information: This includes information about how you use the Service, such as the pages you visit and the features you use.

Technical information: This includes information about your device, such as your IP address and browser type.

How Do We Use Your Personal Information?

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

To provide the Service to you. We use your contact information to send you updates about the Service and to bill you for your subscription. We use your account information to authenticate you and to keep your account secure. We use your usage information to improve the Service and to provide you with personalized recommendations. We use your technical information to troubleshoot problems with the Service and to protect the Service from fraud and abuse.

To communicate with you. We may use your contact information to send you messages about the Service, such as updates, notifications, and support messages. We may also use your contact information to send you marketing messages, such as newsletters and promotional offers.

To improve the Service. We use your usage information to improve the Service and to provide you with personalized recommendations. We use your technical information to troubleshoot problems with the Service and to protect the Service from fraud and abuse.

How Do We Protect Your Personal Information?

We take the security of your personal information seriously. We use a variety of security measures to protect your personal information, including:

Physical security measures: We store your personal information on secure servers in a controlled environment.

Technical security measures: We use encryption and other security measures to protect your personal information when it is transmitted over the internet.

Access controls: We restrict access to your personal information to authorized employees and contractors who need to access it in order to provide the Service.

Your Rights

You have the following rights with respect to your personal information:

Access: You have the right to request access to your personal information.

Correction: You have the right to request that we correct any inaccuracies in your personal information.

Deletion: You have the right to request that we delete your personal information.

Portability: You have the right to request that we provide you with a copy of your personal information in a portable format.

Objection: You have the right to object to the processing of your personal information
