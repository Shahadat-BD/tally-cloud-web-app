Privacy Policy

Introduction

Tally Cloud is dedicated to safeguarding your privacy. This Privacy Statement elucidates the manner in which we gather, utilize, and distribute your personal data when engaging with our website and utilizing our services (referred to as the 'Service').

What Information of a Personal Nature is Gathered by Us?

We gather the subsequent categories of personal details from you :

Contact details : This encompass your name, email, and contact number.

Account particulars : This involve your user ID, passcode, and payment details.

Utilization details : This encompass data on your interaction with the service, such as visited pages and utilized features.

Technical specifics : This consist of details about your device, like IP address and browser category.

How is Your Personal Information Utilized?

We utilize your personal data for the following objectives :

To deliver the Service to you, we utilize your contact details for sending Service updates and subscription billing. Your account information is employed for authentication and ensuring the security of your account. Usage data is analyzed to enhance the Service and offer personalized suggestions. Technical information is applied to troubleshoot Service issues and safeguard against fraud and misuse.

To interact with you, we may utilize your contact details for conveying information about the Service, such as updates, notifications, and support messages. Additionally, your contact information may be employed to dispatch promotional content, including newsletters and special offers.

To enhance the Service, we utilize your usage data to improve performance and offer personalized suggestions. Your technical information is employed to address issues with the Service and safeguard it against fraudulent activities.

How Is Your Personal Data Safeguarded?

We prioritize the safeguarding of your personal data, employing a range of security protocols to ensure its protection, including :

Physical security precautions : Your personal data is safeguarded on secure servers within a controlled setting.

Technological security safeguards : Encryption and additional security protocols shield your personal information during internet transmission.

Access management : We limit entry to your personal details to authorized staff and contractors essential for Service provision.

Your Entitlements/Rights

You are entitled to the subsequent privileges regarding your personal information :

Access : You are entitled to seek access to your personal data.

Correction : You are entitled to request rectification of any inaccuracies in your personal data.

Deletion : You are entitled to request erasure of your personal data.

Portability : You are entitled to request a copy of your personal data in a portable format.

Objection : You are entitled to object to the processing of your personal data.

How to contact us

If there are any inquiries regarding this Privacy Policy, feel free to reach out to us at : info@TallyCloud.com
